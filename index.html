Ukrainian concert in Lisdoonvarna

We are Ukrainian refugees who have taken refuge in Lisdoonvarna. Since our arrival we have received a lot of help from Irish volunteers. And this festival is our way of saying Thank you. We have prepared a small entertainment program for you. Please leave your contact details so that we can see how many people will come. The entrance is free.

Thank you!
